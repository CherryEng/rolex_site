Land‑Dweller

Reinventing the present to lay foundations for the future

A calibre with a revolutionary escapement. A bracelet that harmoniously extends the case.

Unveiled in 2025, the Land-Dweller presents a unique vision of modern watchmaking. With this model, Rolex has opened new horizons, both technical and aesthetic, looking resolutely to the future.

The Land-Dweller

Innovation in motion

To power the Land-Dweller, Rolex has entirely developed and manufactured a new cutting-edge movement: calibre 7135.

Discover the features

Origins of the perfect balance

The Land-Dweller's aesthetic reinterprets the style of watches with integrated bracelets, explored by Rolex since the late 1960s.

  • Striking balance

    This is the ceramic balance staff of the Land-Dweller’s calibre 7135. It is crafted from a patented, exclusive material to ensure resistance to high magnetic fields.

    Discover more

  • In complete continuity

    This is our Flat Jubilee bracelet. Introduced in 2025 on the Land-Dweller, it seamlessly integrates with the case.

    Discover more

  • Beyond the visible

    This is our transparent case back. Today, it is featured on the Land-Dweller and the 1908, as well as on certain versions of the Cosmograph Daytona in 950 platinum.

    Discover more

  • A new impulse

    This is our high-performance Dynapulse escapement. Introduced in 2025 on the Land‑Dweller’s calibre 7135, it transmits energy to the oscillator in a smooth and rhythmic dance.
